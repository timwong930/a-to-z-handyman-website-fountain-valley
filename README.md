# A to Z Handyman Service website starter

## Public business snapshot
- **Company:** A to Z Handyman Service
- **Location:** Fountain Valley, CA
- **Category:** Handyman
- **Website:** http://www.handymanfountainvalley.com/
- **Social:** none found in the public scan

## What they do
A local handyman service focused on small home repairs and maintenance in Fountain Valley and nearby Orange County neighborhoods.

## Why this looks like a good opportunity
- The current site feels like a generic template/parking page with a GoDaddy domain-search link visible in the markup.
- The homepage copy is very thin and the brand presentation is not locally differentiated.
- A better version could add trust signals, a real service-area structure, and a simple quote form.

## Suggested outreach / redesign angle
Lead with a local-SEO and trust refresh: replace the generic landing page with a real Fountain Valley brand, clearer service pages, and a quick-contact flow.

## Starter site included in this repo
This repo includes a simple, responsive starter site in `index.html` and `styles.css` built only from public information.

### Draft structure
- **Home repairs** — Small fixes, quick touch-ups, and everyday maintenance.
- **Interior updates** — Painting, drywall patching, shelving, and furniture assembly.
- **Service area** — Fountain Valley plus nearby Orange County neighborhoods.
- **Estimate** — A simple call or form that makes it easy to request help.
